About Us

Quality, Craftsmanship, Innovation: The Heart of Our Expertise

Golden Frog has been a leader in the leather goods industry since 2009. With a focus on quality, craftsmanship, and sustainable production, we deliver premium leather products, including bags, wallets, and accessories for men and women. Discover the excellence in every stitch.

We are dedicated to producing the finest leather goods since 2009. Our mission is built on quality, innovation, and sustainable practices. We take pride in our zero-tolerance policy on harassment and discrimination, fostering a positive and productive work environment.

A Global Brand with Local Expertise

At Golden Frog, we take pride in being a global leader in leather manufacturing, trusted by renowned brands around the world. Our products are exported to key markets in Japan, Italy, Spain, Kuwait, and beyond, where our commitment to quality and craftsmanship is highly valued. While our reach is global, we stay rooted in the expertise and tradition of Bangladeshi leather craftsmanship, ensuring each product reflects the unique heritage and skill of our local artisans.

By combining local expertise with global market demands, we deliver leather goods that meet international standards while preserving the authenticity of traditional craftsmanship. This blend of global vision and local talent allows us to cater to a diverse clientele, offering personalized service and innovative solutions without compromising on the heritage and quality that define us.

Production Process

    Business Discussion

    Before making a sample, firstly discuss the sample’s design, the leather type and other materials, metal accessories, etc. which will be used for the sample.

  • precision_manufacturing

    Making Samples

    According to the discussion, choose the suitable materials that meet the requirements of the clients, from the rich materials, and make samples in the sample room.

  • diversity_2

    Revision & Discussion

    Discuss the modification of the first samples together with clients.

  • diversity_2

    Sample Complete

    Make the final sample according to the modification.

  • diversity_2

    Quotation & Contract

    Quotation will be provided based on the final sample. After final sample confirmation, the contract related procedures will proceed.

  • diversity_2

    Raw Material

    Producing leather from Bangladesh or Italy. Purchasing lining, metals, reinforcement from China and export to Bangladesh factory.

  • diversity_2

    Bulk Production

    Cutting and inspect the panels. Skiving，glue, inspect the sewing and other processes to produce the products.

  • diversity_2

    Inspection

    Strictly inspect the products according to the prescribed standards,By our internal inspection and third party.
